About the CPJ

For over thirty years, the CPJ has understood and recognized that every student has a voice. Some don't use it, some use it but aren't heard, and some are heard but need another outlet. The Cooper Point Journal serves the purpose of being that outlet. As a student at Evergreen, you can write for the CPJ. You can draw. You can shoot photos. You can comment on others' contributions. You can make suggestions for the design and function of the publication. You can learn behind the scenes, editing or helping us with our (new!) website.
